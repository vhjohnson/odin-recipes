Basic Crepes

Description

This is a simple (but delicious) crepe batter which can be made in minutes.

It's made from ingredients that everyone has on hand.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
